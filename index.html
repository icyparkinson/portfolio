About Me

Hi! I'm a Seattle-based software engineer who primarily uses JavaScript to improve systems, solve problems, and create joy. My passion for learning and helping others led me to earning a Doctorate of Pharmacy degree at the University of Washington. As a pharmacist of seven years, I was struck by how even small improvements in systems and processes can drastically improve a person's quality of life and happiness.

Now, I have redirected my focus to programming, a field in which the learning never stops and where my growth is limitless. I can expand my reach and help even more people through the power of technology.

My tech stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, React, MongoDB
